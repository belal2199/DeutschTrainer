Design a clean, modern neural network visualization used inside a learning app to represent a student’s progress.

The network starts minimal and sparse, with only a few soft, thin nodes and connections. As the student studies more, the neural network gradually becomes stronger, denser, and more complex:

• Nodes increase in number and size
• Connections multiply and grow thicker
• Lines glow softly, indicating learning activity
• The network expands organically, like a living brain

Use a calm, motivating color palette (deep blues, purples, and soft neon highlights).

Animate growth subtly: nodes pulse, connections light up, and new paths fade in smoothly as progress increases.

The final state should feel intelligent, powerful, and alive, clearly communicating mastery and progress without overwhelming the user.

Style: minimal, futuristic, educational, premium UX
Background: soft gradient or dark mode–friendly
Mood: progress, intelligence, growth, focus
